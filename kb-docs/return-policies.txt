========================================
RETURN & REFUND POLICIES
========================================

--- Electronics Return Policy ---
Category: Electronics
Products covered: Smart Watch, Mechanical Keyboard, 4K Webcam, and all other electronics
Return window: 30 days from date of delivery
Conditions:
  - Item must be in original packaging
  - Item must be unused or verified defective
  - All original accessories and manuals must be included
  - Proof of purchase (order confirmation email or receipt) required
Refund method: Full refund to original payment method
Processing time: 5-7 business days after item is received and inspected
Exceptions:
  - Items with physical damage caused by the customer are not eligible
  - Software or digital products bundled with electronics are non-refundable once activated

--- Accessories Return Policy ---
Category: Accessories
Products covered: Laptop Stand, USB-C Hub, Wireless Charging Pad, and all other accessories
Return window: 14 days from date of delivery
Conditions:
  - Item must be in original packaging, unused
  - All original accessories must be included
  - Proof of purchase required
Refund method: Store credit or exchange only (no cash refund)
Processing time: 3-5 business days after item is received
Exceptions:
  - Personalized or custom-engraved accessories are final sale
  - Clearance accessories are exchange-only

--- Audio Return Policy ---
Category: Audio
Products covered: Wireless Headphones, Portable Bluetooth Speaker, and all other audio products
Return window: 30 days from date of delivery
Conditions:
  - Full refund available within first 15 days, no questions asked
  - After 15 days, only defective items are eligible for replacement
  - Item must be in reasonable condition (original packaging preferred but not required for defective claims)
  - Proof of purchase required
Refund method: Full refund within 15 days; replacement unit after 15 days
Processing time: 5-7 business days for refunds, 7-10 business days for replacements
Exceptions:
  - Ear tips and replacement pads are non-returnable for hygiene reasons

--- General Return Information ---
Shipping:
  - Free return shipping for defective items
  - Customer pays return shipping for non-defective returns ($5.99 flat rate)
  - Return shipping label provided via email after return request is approved

How to initiate a return:
  1. Contact customer support with your order number
  2. Describe the reason for return
  3. Receive a return authorization number (RA#) and shipping label
  4. Ship the item within 7 days of receiving the RA#
  5. Refund or exchange processed after inspection

Warranty claims:
  - Warranty claims are separate from returns
  - Contact support with product ID and proof of purchase for warranty service
  - Warranty covers manufacturing defects only, not accidental damage
